☟ shot on iphone

instagram: @milla.snow

a large part of my creative output has become iPhone photography. the portable aspect of the device allows me to take photos at random times and to capture very brief moments in time. i find that i often prefer it to digital and analog photography because there's much less control and process involved; it becomes much more focused on a split second of time than fine tuning and the machine being used. i've also come to appreciate square formatting of photography; when put together, it creates a certain coherence of my own various memories.
